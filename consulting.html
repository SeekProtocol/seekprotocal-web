What is the Regional Wall Map Project?

Our Regional Wall Maps are large, printed maps that display key geographical areas, including towns, landmarks, and essential services, in your region. We work closely with local councils and the region’s most prominent brands to create these maps, which are then distributed across public spaces to ensure widespread visibility.

Printed & Delivered

We print high-quality, durable maps and ensure they are distributed in strategic locations.

Strategic Placement

Maps are placed in high-traffic public locations like train stations, bus stations, hospitals, gyms, and local councils, offering unique exposure.

Long-Term Impact

Each map lasts for 5 years, ensuring long-term brand presence and ongoing visibility.

Local Engagement

Your brand becomes part of a regional project that helps with local development and fosters community pride.
